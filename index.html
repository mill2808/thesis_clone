Welcome

Solarfordairy is a tool that aims to support dairy farmers as they experience increasing costs of production and stagnant milk prices. This program aims to help the dairy industry achieve its goal of net zero carbon emissions by 2050 and hopes to protect dairy farmers from the market volatility of increasing electricity prices.

The Michigan Farm Energy Audit Program invites you to learn more about potential savings on-farm through the installation of solar technology. Using your energy audit information through our program, or the information provided on your electricity bill, you can estimate the cost of installing solar panels on your farm.

Solar panels are meant to be installed on non-arable land, roofs, or be dual-functioning by providing shade for your cows during the hot summer months. Please proceed to the cost calculator for more information.
