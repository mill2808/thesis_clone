Solar for Dairy

Solar for Dairy is a program designed to help dairy farmers in Michigan explore the potential of installing solar panels on their farm structures and non-arable farmland.

The Investment Tax Credit

The Investment Tax Credit (ITC), also known as the federal solar tax credit allows The investment tax credit (ITC), also known as the federal solar tax credit, applies to both residential and commercial projects. It allows you to deduct a percentage of the cost of installation from your federal taxes.

2020 - 2022: Owners of the new residential and commercial solar project can deduct 26% of the installation cost from their taxes

2023: Owners of the new residential and commercial solar project can deduct 22% of the installation cost from their taxes

2024: Owners of the commercial solar project can deduct 10% of the cost from their taxes
